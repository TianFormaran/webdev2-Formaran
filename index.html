About Us

University band

Kogan x Aulasy is a dynamic university band hailing from BathSpa University RAK. With a powerhouse lineup of eight members, their music fuses the electrifying sounds of pop-punk and metal, creating a high-energy sonic experience. But that's not all within this musical powerhouse exists Aulasy, a captivating sub-band spinning indie and pop melodies that leave a lasting impression.
